Deploying project with Netlify

    1. Go to Netfly.com
    2. Create a new account and signIn.
    3. Go to "Sites".
    4. Click on "Import from git".
    5. Choose "github" option.
    6. Authorize permissions for accessing Netfly to github repository.
    7. Choose the project and click on that.
    8. Add information required by Netfly like: site name, branch to deploy (in this case main) and base directory.
    9. Click on "Deploy site".
    10. Done!! you have deployed your web page to a Prod environment.
